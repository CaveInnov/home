About Cav-e-innoV

Engineering the Future of Sensing

Cav-e-innoV is a deep-tech startup focused on developing cavity-enabled sensing technologies and advanced embedded systems. Our solutions enable ultra-precise measurements and intelligent data processing across multiple domains.

Incubated at IIT Delhi, we bridge fundamental research with real-world deployment, delivering scalable and high-performance sensing platforms for industry, healthcare, and robotics.

Why Choose Us?

We combine expertise in optics, electronics, and embedded intelligence to build next-generation sensing systems. Our solutions are designed for precision, scalability, and real-time performance.

From lab-scale innovation to deployable products, Cav-e-innoV ensures reliability and cutting-edge performance.
